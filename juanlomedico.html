I always dreamed working as a developer but having obligations with my family I had to stay in a steady well payed job. After a car accident in 2006 I was told I couldn't continue the pace that hospitality. This is what actually open the doors for me to finally learn and be involved in the development field and finally be happy in a carrer of my choice.
